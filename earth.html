About Us

Radial and Cross Ply Tyres is a tyre servicing company. We specialize in the supply and delivery of brand new tyres of all sizes ranging from passenger tyres, truck and bus tyres, tractor tyres and earth mover tyres. We also do repairs and retreading. We can survey your fleet, determine the condition of its maintenance, and recommend corrections for better management and ultimately lower cost per kilometer.
